Indian Sign Language

SignSense is an innovative website that utilizes machine learning to recognize hand signs in Indian Sign Language (ISL) and predict corresponding words. By serving as a bridge between deaf and hearing individuals, SignSense aims to enhance communication and foster inclusivity. With its advanced ML models and computer vision algorithms, SignSense enables real-time recognition of ISL gestures, allowing for seamless interaction and understanding. To get started, simply click the button bellow, turn on your device's camera, and begin signing. Letter - A

The letter "A" in Indian Sign Language is represented by forming a fist and extending the thumb while keeping the fingers touching the thumb. The extended thumb represents the vertical stroke of the letter "A" in the written form.

Letter - B

The letter "B" in Indian Sign Language is represented by holding your hand open with all fingers extended and touching together. Then, bend your thumb inward towards your palm. The fingertips of the thumb and index finger should be touching. The remaining fingers should be extended and touching each other. The handshape resembles the written letter "B".
